Chapter 1: Extend Your Knowledge

Guideline 1.1: Provide alt text for non-text content such as images, charts, or audio so that users with visual impairments or assistive technologies can still understand the information.

Guideline 1.2: Make sure audio and video content is accessible by including captions, transcripts, or audio descriptions. This makes sure people who are deaf, hard of hearing, or blind can still access the information.

Guideline 1.3: Create content that can be presented in different ways without losing meaning. This means using proper structure, headings, and semantic HTML instead of relying only on visual styling.

Guideline 1.4: Make content easy to see and hear by using clear contrast between text and background, avoiding color as the only way to convey meaning, and allowing users to control audio or zoom without losing content.
